Orijinz Daily Covers

How do you play?

You are trying to guess the original artist of a song covered by another musician. Click/Tap ▶ Tap PLAY to listen the cover. Your timer starts when you press any button. Spotify will play up to 30 seconds of the cover for free; if you are logged into Spotify you may be able to listen to more.

What is Reveal a Letter?

Click/Tap 🔡 Reveal a Letter and a random letter from the original artist's name is shown. You can reveal as many letters as you like — even the full answer if you're stuck.

What is Reveal Song Name?

Tap 🔍 Reveal Song Name to unmask the song title and the cover artist. A very useful hint if hearing the cover alone isn't enough.

How is my score calculated?

Lower is better. Score = your time + 15 seconds per letter revealed + 30 seconds if Reveal Song Name is used + 5 seconds per wrong letter guessed.

Where do I see everyone's scores?

After you play, tap Results to view your results and the results of other players on the Orijinz leaderboard. There are three tabs — Score, Games Played, and Streak (games played in a row). If you click the arrows next to "Today's Results" you can see the outcomes for each of the past three days.

Can I remove my name from the Leaderboard?

Yes! Many players just want to play for fun without worrying about scores. To remove your name from the leaderboard, click the menu, choose "Settings" and toggle off "Show me on the leaderboard."

Can I change my Leaderboard display name?

Yes. To change your name, go to the menu on the upper left and select "Account Settings." Tap the "Display name" input box, change your name and then tap the "Update Info" button.

Can I share my results with others?

A key part of the fun of Orijinz Daily is sharing your results with others, convincing them to play, comparing your results, and discussing the clues. Next to Results is Share! — tap it to share your results.

Can I view my statistics over time?

Yes, you can view and share your stats. Click on the menu on the upper left of the screen and choose "Statistics." You can see your stats for the game you are playing on the default "This Game" tab. Select the "Today" tab to see the results from the games you have played today or the "All Games" tab to see your stats across all games you have subscribed to.

Is there a way to play earlier puzzles?

Unfortunately not. You get one new puzzle every day.

Can I use the native keyboard on my mobile device?

Yes, if the Orijinz Daily mobile keyboard is hard for you to use, you can try the native keyboard built into your device. Go to the hamburger menu in the upper left and choose Settings and enable the native keyboard. Note that some native keyboards require extra scrolling and can be awkward. Unfortunately, this is how they are designed.

Can we send you feedback or ideas?

Of course — email orijinzgames@outlook.com.
