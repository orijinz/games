Orijinz Daily 70s Songs

One of the many fun and fascinating Orijinz games!

How do you play?

Read the clue about a hit song from the 70s and guess the song title by typing in your answer — a timer starts when you begin.

What is the Listen button?

Tap "Listen" to hear a clip of the song. Choose 10, 20, or 30 seconds — you can pick any duration and listen as many times as you like. Each second of listening adds 2 seconds to your score.

What is Reveal a Letter?

Tap "Reveal a Letter" and a random letter from the answer is shown. You can reveal as many letters as you like — even the full answer if you're stuck. Each reveal adds 15 seconds to your score.

How is my score calculated?

Lower is better. Score = your time + 2× seconds listened + 15 seconds per letter revealed + 5 seconds per wrong letter guessed.

Where do I see everyone's scores?

After you play, tap the "Results" button to view your results and the results of other players on the Orijinz leaderboard. There are three tabs — Score, Games Played, and Streak (Games played in a row). Tap the "Share" button to share your results with friends or family. Want to have fun and not worry about your score? You can remove your name from the leaderboard. Click the "Hamburger" three-line menu, choose "Settings" and toggle off "Show me on the leaderboard."

Can I view my statistics over time?

Yes — click the "Hamburger" three-line menu and choose "Statistics."

Is there a way to play earlier puzzles?

Unfortunately, not. If you want access to lots more, get the full Orijinz App.

Can we send you feedback or ideas?

Of course — email orijinzgames@outlook.com.
