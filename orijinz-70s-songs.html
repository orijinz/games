Help

Orijinz Daily 70s Songs

How do you play?

Read the song title from a hit song from the 70s and guess the artist by typing in your answer — a timer starts when you begin.

What is the Listen button?

Tap 🎧 Listen to hear a clip of the song. Choose 10, 20, or 30 seconds and listen as many times as you like.

What is Reveal a Letter?

Tap 🔡 Reveal a Letter and a random letter from the answer is shown. You can reveal as many letters as you like — even the full answer if you're stuck.

How is my score calculated?

Lower is better. Score = your time + 2× seconds listened + 15 seconds per letter revealed + 5 seconds per wrong letter guessed.

Where do I see everyone's scores?

After you play, tap the Results to view your results and the results of other players on the Orijinz leaderboard. There are three tabs — Score, Games Played, and Streak (Games played in a row). Tap the Share! to share your results with friends or family.

Can I change my name or remove my name from the Leaderboard?

Yes. To change your name go to the menu on the upper left and select "Account Settings." Tap the "Display name" input box, change your name and then tap the "Update Info" button. To remove your name from the leaderboard, click the menu, choose "Settings" and toggle off "Show me on the leaderboard."

Can I view my statistics over time?

Yes, you can view and share your stats. Click on the menu on the upper left of the screen and choose "Statistics."

Is there a way to play earlier puzzles?

Unfortunately not. You get one new puzzle every day.

Can I use the native keyboard on my mobile device?

Yes, if the Orijinz Daily mobile keyboard is hard for you to use, you can try the native keyboard built into your device. Go to the hamburger menu in the upper left and choose Settings and enable the native keyboard. Note that some native keyboards require extra scrolling and can be awkward. Unfortunately, this is how they are designed.

Can we send you feedback or ideas?

Of course — email orijinzgames@outlook.com.
