Orijinz Quotes

One of the many fun and fascinating Orijinz games!

How do you play?

Read the famous quote and guess who said it or the movie/TV show it came from by typing in your answer — a timer starts when you begin.

What is the Hint button?

Tap "Hint" to get an extra clue about who said the quote. Using the hint adds 30 seconds to your score.

What is Reveal a Letter?

Tap "Reveal a Letter" and a random letter from the answer is shown. You can reveal as many letters as you like — even the full answer if you're stuck. Each reveal adds 15 seconds to your score.

How is my score calculated?

Lower is better. Score = your time + 30 seconds for using the hint + 15 seconds per letter revealed + 5 seconds per wrong guess.

Where do I see everyone's scores?

After you play tap the "Results" button to view your results and the results of other players on the Orijinz leaderboard. There are three tabs — Score, Games Played, and Streak (Games played in a row). Tap the "Share" button to share your results with friends or family. To remove your name from the leaderboard, click the "Hamburger" three-line menu, choose "Settings" and toggle off "Show me on the leaderboard."

Can I view my statistics over time?

Yes — click the "Hamburger" three-line menu and choose "Statistics."

Is there a way to play earlier puzzles?

Unfortunately, not. If you want access to lots more, get the full Orijinz App.

Can we send you feedback or ideas?

Of course — email orijinzgames@outlook.com.
